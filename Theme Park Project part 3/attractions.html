Get ready to ride!

SkyBlaze

SkyBlaze is a one-of-a-kind thrill ride that takes you on a soaring adventure through the clouds.

Strap yourself into your seat and hold on tight as you're lifted high into the air, soaring higher and higher until you're above the treetops. Then, get ready for the ride of your life as you plummet back down to the ground, twisting and turning through the air at incredible speeds.

With hair-raising drops and heart-stopping twists and turns, SkyBlaze is a ride that's not for the faint of heart. Are you ready to take on the challenge?

ThunderBolt

Get ready for a thunderous adventure on Thunderbolt, the ultimate roller coaster experience.

Brace yourself as you're launched from the starting gate, hurtling through a series of twisting turns and sudden drops. Feel the wind in your hair and the rush of adrenaline as you speed through the course at breakneck speeds, dodging obstacles and defying gravity.

With lightning-fast acceleration and heart-pumping thrills, Thunderbolt is a ride that will leave you breathless.

AquaRush

Dive into an aquatic adventure on AquaRush, the most exciting water ride around.

Climb aboard your raft and get ready to brave the rapids, as you're whisked down a winding water course. Feel the refreshing spray of water as you navigate through twists and turns, dodging obstacles and surging over drops.

With unexpected surprises and plenty of thrills along the way, AquaRush is a ride that will leave you soaking wet and begging for more.
